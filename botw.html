The legend of zelda: Breath of The Wild

Rated E+10
Action adventure, A-RPG, Puzzle, Open world.
2017
50 Hours
9,7

Link, who awakens from a deep slumber to a mysterious voice urging him to rescue Princess Zelda and save the kingdom of Hyrule from the ancient evil known as Calamity Ganon. The game introduces an expansive open world where Link can explore various regions, solve puzzles, battle enemies, and uncover the rich history of Hyrule. With the aid of the Sheikah Slate and allies he encounters along the way, Link embarks on a quest to defeat Calamity Ganon and restore peace to the land. The game is renowned for its innovative open-world design and emphasis on player freedom and exploration.
